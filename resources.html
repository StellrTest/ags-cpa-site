How to Pay Your AGS CPA Invoice

Client Portal

The easiest way to pay your invoice is through our secure online client portal. Log in using the Client Portal link provided in your engagement email to view and pay your invoice.

E-Transfer

Send e-transfers to info@agscpa.ca. Please include your invoice number or client name in the message field so we can apply your payment correctly.

Cheque

Cheques can be made payable to AGS CPA Professional Corporation and mailed to:

3800 Steeles Avenue West, Unit 400
Vaughan, Ontario L4L 4G9

Credit Card

We accept Visa and Mastercard payments by phone. Please contact our office directly to process your payment securely over the phone.

Wire Transfer

For wire transfer instructions, please contact our office directly at info@agscpa.ca and we will provide the necessary banking details.

Still Need Help?
