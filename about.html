About the Firm

Our Story

A firm built on trust, precision, and results

AGS CPA Professional Corporation is a Vaughan-based accounting firm serving individuals, professionals, and growing businesses across the Greater Toronto Area. We combine advanced academic credentials with a hands-on, partner-led approach that ensures every client receives the attention and expertise their financial situation deserves.

  • 01
    Personalized Service Every engagement is handled with care and direct partner involvement — not passed off to junior staff. You always know who is working on your file.
  • 02
    Advanced Expertise CPA and MAcc credentials backed by deep expertise in Canadian tax law, financial reporting, corporate structuring, and strategic business advisory.
  • 03
    Technology-Forward As a Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor, we leverage modern accounting tools to give you real-time financial visibility, accurate records, and actionable insights.
