What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive services tailored to your financial goals.

From individual tax returns to full corporate accounting, we deliver expert, partner-led service at every stage of your financial journey — with the precision and personal attention you deserve.

01 — Accounting

Financial Accounting & Reporting

Accurate, timely financial statements give you the clarity to make informed decisions and satisfy regulatory requirements. We handle the full accounting cycle — from journal entries through to year-end financial statements.

  • Year-end financial statement preparation
  • Monthly and quarterly management reporting
  • Notice-to-Reader and Review Engagements
  • CRA audit support and representation
  • Financial analysis and benchmarking
02 — Bookkeeping

Bookkeeping & Payroll

Clean, organized books are the foundation of every well-run business. As a Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor, we maintain accurate records and handle payroll so you can focus on what you do best.

  • Full-cycle bookkeeping (monthly or quarterly)
  • QuickBooks setup, training, and ongoing support
  • Payroll processing and CRA remittances
  • HST / GST filing and reconciliation
  • Bank and credit card reconciliation
03 — Tax

Tax Planning & Filing

We go beyond simply filing returns — we proactively plan to minimize your tax burden while keeping you fully compliant with CRA requirements. Our expertise covers both personal and corporate taxation.

  • Personal tax return preparation (T1)
  • Corporate tax return preparation (T2)
  • Tax planning and income-splitting strategies
  • CRA correspondence and dispute resolution
  • SR&ED credits and government incentives
  • Estate and trust taxation
04 — Advisory

Business Consulting & Advisory

Sound financial strategy is the difference between surviving and thriving. We act as your trusted advisor — helping you structure your business, plan for growth, and make confident, data-driven decisions.

  • Business incorporation and corporate structuring
  • Cash flow forecasting and budgeting
  • Business acquisition due diligence
  • Succession planning and exit strategies
  • Financial performance review and KPI analysis
